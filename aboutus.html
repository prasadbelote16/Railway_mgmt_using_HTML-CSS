Indian Railways (IR) is a statutory body under the jurisdiction of Ministry of Railways,
Government of India that operates India's national railway system.
It manages the fourth-largest railway network in the world by size,
with a route length of 67,956 km (42,226 mi) as of 31 March 2020.
